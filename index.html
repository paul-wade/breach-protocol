How to Play

Educational Objectives

This simulation teaches AI safety concepts through interactive scenarios:

  • AI Alignment: Learn how AI systems can misinterpret objectives
  • Human Oversight: Practice maintaining control over AI decisions
  • Risk Assessment: Identify potential failure modes
  • Safety Measures: Implement proper AI safety protocols

Controls

  • Pause AI: Stop AI processing to review decisions
  • Review Decision: Examine AI reasoning before proceeding
  • Override AI: Make your own decision when AI suggests unsafe actions
